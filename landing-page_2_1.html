Money-Saving Strategies

30+ Ways to Travel
for Less

✈ Flights
Book 6–8 Weeks Ahead
The sweet spot for international flight pricing — not too early, not last minute. Flexible dates save an additional 20–40%.
Save 20–40%
💳 Banking
Always Choose Local Currency
When ATMs ask "home currency or local?" — always choose local. The Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) option costs you 3–7% extra every single time.
Save 3–7% per withdrawal
🏨 Accommodation
Night Buses = Free Accommodation
Overnight buses and trains cover both transport and a night's accommodation in one. In SE Asia this saves $15–30 per journey.
Save $15–30 per trip
🍜 Food
Eat Where Locals Eat
If there's an English menu outside, expect tourist prices. Walk one street back from main attractions and pay 50–80% less for the same quality.
Save 50–80%
💱 Money
Use Wise or Revolut
Zero (or near-zero) FX conversion fees on every transaction. The single most important financial tool for any international traveller.
Save hundreds per trip
🏨 Accommodation
Monthly Airbnb Rates
Committing to monthly Airbnb stays unlocks 30–50% discounts compared to nightly rates. Perfect for slow travel and longer stays in one city.
Save 30–50%
